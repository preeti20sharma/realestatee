About Company

Real estate in India is exploding.

Real estate in India is booming, but genuine buyers are scarce. Billboards and expos won’t help if your leads don’t convert. That’s where The Intelligent Realtors come in. Based in Lucknow and led by industry expert Mr. Ashwani Kumar Singh, we specialize in real estate lead generation—connecting builders, brokers, and investors with serious buyers across India. From luxury apartments in Gurgaon to commercial hubs in Hyderabad, our campaigns deliver exclusive, verified, and conversion-ready leads—not just inquiries.

How We Generate High-Quality Real Estate Leads

At The Intelligent Realtors, we don’t rely on guesswork. We combine local real estate expertise with advanced digital marketing strategies to deliver leads that are exclusive, verified, and sales-ready.

Our Proven Real Estate Lead Generation Channels:

Google Ads

(Search & Display)

Capture high-intent property buyers actively searching for residential and commercial real estate in India.

Meta Ads

(Facebook & Instagram)

Use carousel, video, and retargeting ads to build awareness, nurture interest, and drive conversions.

YouTube Campaigns

(Video Ads)

Immersive property walkthroughs and video ads that showcase your projects like a digital site visit.

LinkedIn Ads

( Built for HNI & NRI)

Perfect for commercial projects, pre-leased properties, and HNI/NRI investor targeting.

Data + CRM Integration

Real-time lead delivery directly to your sales team, ensuring no delay in follow-ups and higher closure rates.
